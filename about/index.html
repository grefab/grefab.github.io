Gregor Fabritius

Born in 1982 in Transylvania and raised in Westerwald, Gregor studied computer science and a bit of psychology in Aachen before his profession took him to Berlin around 2010. Once there, he initially worked as a project engineer and later as a freelancer, before taking over Isotronic GmbH in 2017, which he has been managing ever since.
